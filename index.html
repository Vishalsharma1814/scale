Golden Goose

Cloud-Based Bookkeeping Firm in Toronto, Canada


At Golden Goose, we truly believe that bookkeeping provides the freedom to small business owners from tasks that distract from running the core business. We also believe that Cloud technology has opened up new ways to better serve small businesses, ensuring you keep your overhead costs down, and have access to up to date information about your business when and how you want it.

We are firmly committed to leveraging the best Cloud-Based technology to provide maximum value and maximum efficiency. We use automation to speed up data entry while providing a paperless and ultra-reliable audit-proofing record-keeping method that links every transaction to the source document.

Our Toronto based Digital Bookkeeping Firm offers services to clients across Halton Hills, Milton, Burlington, Oakville, Caledon, Brampton, Mississauga, King, Vaughan, Markham, Newmarket, Aurora, Richmond Hill, Georgina, Uxbridge, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, Greater Toronto Area, Toronto, Ottawa, Hamilton, Kitchener, Cambridge, Waterloo, London, St. Catharines, Niagara, Oshawa, Windsor, Barrie, Kingston, Guelph, Brantford, Peterborough, Southern Ontario and the rest of Canada.
