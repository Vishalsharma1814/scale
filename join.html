JOIN THE TEAM

Golden Goose is always on the lookout for smart and friendly bookkeepers, with Canadian experience, wanting a more exciting career than those found in a traditional firm:

✔ Work Remotely

✔ Cloud based environment

✔ Focused on tech start-ups

✔ Flexible Hours


Sounds Interesting? If you meet the criteria below, we definitely want to hear from you!

Experience:

✔ Tech Savvy - You know your way around QuickBooks Online and Xero and you have amazing troubleshooting skills. In addition to QBO and Xero, we use other online applications such as Receipt Bank, Xero Practice Manager, Hubdoc, LastPass, and other client specific platforms so you should definitely be comfortable learning and using new technology with minimum supervision.

✔ Advanced Excel skills - We do a lot (really, a LOT!) of manipulation in Excel. Although we have created working papers templates to streamline our processes, these templates sometimes need tweaks so you should be comfortable making necessary changes without affecting the workbook structure. You should also know how to use vlookup and pivot tables.

✔ Experience handling multi-currency transactions.

✔ Ability to work with multiple companies concurrently.

✔ QuickBooks Online and Xero certification would be a great asset. You can take these certifications for free!.

✔ Solid knowledge of Canadian Payroll and Sales Taxes, as well as experience filing the associated government returns

✔ And last, but not least, you must be proficient in full-cycle accounting.

Skills:

✔ You learn fast and can usually figure things out on your own.

✔ Work under minimum supervision, yet you are a great team player.

✔ Online collaboration is your thing; Google Hangouts is one of your ever running apps.

✔ May sound cliché but in a remote environment like ours, English proficiency is a must; both spoken and written.

✔ Comfortable having one on one meetings with top management.

✔ Organized and ability to prioritize in order to meet deadlines.

✔ Customer service oriented.

What you will need:

✔ You will need a computer in great shape and strong, fast and reliable wifi - we recommend unlimited data plans.

✔ Have a quiet office/space where you can go to when you need to get on a call with a client

✔ Must be eligible to work in Canada


Our clients are mainly based in Ontario but we do have clients in Quebec and a few others across Canada. If you are bilingual (English/French) and have experience in Quebec, please mention it in your application!
